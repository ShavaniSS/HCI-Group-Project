  • Personal Goals

Establish a development plan with the employee that helps the individual grow professinally in ways important to him or her. The employee should identify ways in which he/she would like to further/develop professional performance; training, assignments, new challenges etc. Examine job responsibilities for the employee for the upcoming period, agree on standards of performance for key job responsibilities, set goals and establish the specific measurement criteria for each goal. These include but are not limited to specific project completion goals, team work related goals, quality and quantity of work expectations, attendance and punctuality
