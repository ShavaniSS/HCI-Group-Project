  • Appraisal Discussion Recommendation Sheet (Completed by the Immediate Next-Level Manager)

The Appraisal Discussion Recommendation Sheet can only be completed by the Immediate Next-Level Manager after he/she has reviewed an employee's Performance Assessment Form. Once the Immediate Next-Level Manager thinks it is in the best interest of the Business Unit then he/she may make changes, either positive or negative, to the employees assessment rating score. Recommendations need to be substantive and specific. For example; Section 2, 'Knowledge of Job', followed by comments and supporting evidence. This serves as a formal recommendation and does not mandate the supervisor to make any changes to the employees form. At no time should this form be seen by an employee or discussed with an employee. The Immediate Next-Level Manager must attach this sheet with the employees Performance Assessment Form before sending the document to the Human Resource Department.
